## Tone Variations

Our voice stays consistent, but tone adapts to context:

| Context | Tone | Example |
|---------|------|---------|
| Errors | Empathetic, helpful | "Couldn't save your changes. Try again?" |
| Success | Celebratory (not over-the-top) | "Adventure created! What's next?" |
| Onboarding | Encouraging, guiding | "Let's set up your first..." |
| Settings | Clear, neutral | "Choose your preferences" |
